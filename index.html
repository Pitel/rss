What does the Rejuvenation Credit buff do in Deadlock? Image via Destructoid. The Rejuvenation Credit buff is given upon killing the Mid Boss, marked by a small golden symbol in the middle of the map. This boss, when killed, drops the Rejuvenator, which you need to heavy melee to secure. Once you do, all living teammates will be granted the Rejuvenation Credit buff for a lengthy period. This buff causes all minions you kill to drop 50% more souls and reduces the timer on your next respawn by half. It also grants increased health regeneration. The Rejuvenation Credit buff is lost upon death, though the respawn reduction applies to this death. It's also worth noting that this buff can be stolen by the enemy team. If your team manages to kill the Mid Boss, but fails to melee the Rejuvenator before the enemies do, they'll receive the buff instead. When should you go for the mid boss and Rejuvenator? Your team should attempt the mid boss either when you have a substantial lead or when several enemy players are dead. If you attempt the Mid Boss while all enemies are up, there's a good chance they could rush into the mid boss room and melt your team. Worse yet, one person could sneak in and steal it.
