This one is super easy to complete: you just need to parry (press the jump button while in the air with Cuphead or Mugman) the spirits before they reach the middle of the arena. They move slowly and there aren't that many to defeat. After that, a ferry NPC appears that brings you to the new Cuphead DLC island After completion the Mausoleum will crumble, denoting that it was conquered. An NPC will appear holding an oar, and their boat will take you to "Island IV," which is the location of the DLC.
